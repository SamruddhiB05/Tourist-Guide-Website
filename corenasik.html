WINE CAPITAL OF INDIA

Pleasant weather, a beautiful city, and warm people – you are sure to fall in love with Pune.
If you dreamed of a place where the right-brained dreamers inspired the left-brained doers – Pune would be the perfect place. Pune is a city where the creative minds of the world live side by side with the scientific and technological innovators of tomorrow. Known as Punya-nagri (city of virtuous deeds), or the Oxford of the East (for its many educational institutions) Pune is a thriving cultural and educational hub and a young, cosmopolitan city. Pune has kept the great traditions of Indian classical music, dance and drama alive and thriving, with performers of international repute and a connoisseur audience. Home to unique events like the Sawai Gandharv Music Festival, Pune International Film Festival, Shaniwar Wada Festival and the Pune Ganesh Festival, it is a city where there is something of interest, serving diverse choices, throughout the year. The city also boasts an eclectic collection of shops, restaurants, coffee houses and a vibrant art scene. Pune holds in its heart a lot of youthful energy and independent spirit with a rich mix of traditional and contemporary beliefs and values. The city welcomes people from far and wide, providing them with a safe ‘home-like’ environment and a large number of international and out station students call this city home. With its multifarious charms, Pune provides the perfect balance of work and play.
